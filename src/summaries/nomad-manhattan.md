NoMad — short for North of Madison Square Park — is a fashionable neighborhood just north of the Flatiron District, roughly bounded by 25th and 30th Streets between Sixth Avenue and Lexington Avenue, that has emerged over the past two decades as one of Manhattan's most dynamic dining and hospitality destinations. Anchored by the historic Madison Square North Historic District, its grand turn-of-the-century commercial buildings have been reimagined as boutique hotels, acclaimed restaurants, and upscale residences.
