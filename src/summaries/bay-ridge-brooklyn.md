Bay Ridge is a residential neighborhood in southwestern Brooklyn, stretching along the Narrows waterfront with stunning views of the Verrazzano-Narrows Bridge. Known for its strong Scandinavian, Irish, and Italian-American heritage, the neighborhood features tree-lined streets of brick homes, a bustling commercial corridor along Third and Fifth Avenues, and the scenic Shore Road promenade. Bay Ridge has maintained a close-knit, family-oriented community atmosphere while becoming increasingly diverse in recent decades.
