Hudson Heights is a residential enclave perched atop the Palisades ridge in upper Manhattan, roughly between 181st and 187th Streets, offering some of the most dramatic views of the Hudson River and the New Jersey Palisades to be found anywhere in the city. The neighborhood is prized for its pre-war apartment buildings, quiet tree-lined streets, and proximity to Fort Tryon Park and The Cloisters, giving it a distinctly serene and village-like atmosphere.
