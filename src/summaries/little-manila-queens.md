Little Manila is a Filipino commercial and cultural corridor along Roosevelt Avenue between 63rd and 71st Streets in Woodside, home to a dense concentration of Filipino restaurants, grocery stores, remittance centers, and shipping services that make it the primary hub of Filipino-American life in the New York metropolitan area. It serves as a vital gathering point for the tri-state Filipino community, where food, language, and cultural ties to the Philippines are maintained through businesses catering to one of Queens' largest immigrant groups.
