Flushing Meadows Corona Park is Queens' largest park and one of the most storied public spaces in the city, having hosted both the 1939 and 1964 World's Fairs, whose iconic Unisphere and New York State Pavilion towers still stand. Today it is home to Citi Field, the USTA Billie Jean King National Tennis Center, the Queens Museum, and the Queens Zoo, making it an unmatched destination for sports, culture, and recreation.
