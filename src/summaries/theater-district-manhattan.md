The Theater District is the heart of New York City's world-famous Broadway theater scene, centered around West 42nd through 54th Streets between Seventh and Eighth Avenues in Midtown Manhattan. Home to dozens of legendary venues including the Shubert, Booth, and Majestic theaters, the neighborhood draws millions of visitors each year to its dazzling marquees and live performances. Times Square sits at its core, making the Theater District one of the most recognizable and vibrant areas in the city.
