North Shore Towers is a self-contained gated residential community of three 33-story condominium towers in Glen Oaks at the Queens-Nassau border, built in the 1970s on the former grounds of the Gross Morton Park Country Club and still featuring an 18-hole golf course, tennis courts, and a shopping arcade on its grounds. Its combination of full amenities, suburban setting, and relative affordability compared to Manhattan high-rises has made it popular with retirees and commuters seeking a suburban lifestyle within city limits.
