Queensboro Hill is a working- and middle-class residential neighborhood in central Flushing, bounded by Kissena Park to the south and the busy commercial strips of Main Street and Kissena Boulevard to the west, with a dense mix of attached homes and small apartment buildings housing a diverse population that has grown increasingly Chinese and South Asian in recent decades. Its location between Flushing's commercial core and the quieter parkland of Kissena gives it a transitional character that is neither fully suburban nor urban.
